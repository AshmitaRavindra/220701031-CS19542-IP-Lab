About The College

1. History

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
It was established in the year 1997 under the ages of Rajalakshmi Educational Trust whose members have had consummate experience in the fields of education and industry. The College has grown from strength to strength in the last 25 years and progressing towards Excellence in Engineering Education, Research and Development.

2.Vision

To be an institution of excellence in Engineering, Technology and Management Education & Research.
To provide competent and ethical professionals with a concern for society.

3.Mission

To impart quality technical education imbibed with proficiency and humane values. To provide right ambience and opportunities for the students to develop into creative, talented and globally competent professionals. To promote research and development in technology and management for the benefit of the society.
