Facilities

1. Administration Services

The Administrative Department of the college is headed by the Administrative Officer. The Administrative Office serves as the backbone of the Institution by handling Student Service, HR, Purchase, Despatch, and all official correspondences with the regulatory bodies such as AICTE, DTE, Anna University, etc.
The department is well computerized with an exclusive file management system for staff and students.

2. Library

The College library is located in a spacious new building of 9000 Sq. ft. The library is open to students and staff members from 7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. on all working days.
The library has a rich collection of around 52,600 volumes of textbooks, reference books, and national and international journals in all branches of Engineering, Science, Technology, Management, and General Studies. The collection is ever increasing.
The books are classified according to the Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme. An open-access system is followed in the library.

3. Transport

The College runs 85 buses to provide transport facilities to students from various places in the city.
The number of buses will be increased corresponding to the increase in the intake of students.

4. IT Infrastructure

The college has more than 2000 Desktop PCs/Laptops on campus in various academic blocks connected through a fibre optic backbone. Internet access is provided to all the computers.
Classrooms and seminar halls are equipped with interactive panels, smart boards, lecture capturing systems, and LCD projectors for delivering presentations.

5. Green Campus

Rajalakshmi Engineering College has adopted many green cover initiatives, enriching the eco-friendly ambience of the campus.
Separate parking facilities are available for two- and four-wheelers for both faculties and students near the entrance.
Security personnel are assigned duties at every turn to ensure restricted entry of automobiles in no-parking areas.

6. Hostel

We have separate hostels for boys and girls. The hostel can accommodate 500 boys and 200 girls. Single room accommodation is provided for final year and PG students. They are also permitted to use their own computers in their rooms.
The mess is spacious with a dining hall and kitchen, and separate seating arrangements are made for boys and girls.
