2. IEEE

Founded in 1884, the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With over 423,000 members in over 160 countries, the IEEE boasts a vibrant global community of engineers, scientists, and tech enthusiasts.
The IEEE's impact is undeniable. From setting industry standards to shaping public policy, the IEEE plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology.
Its commitment to innovation, education, and global collaboration has fostered advancements that improve our lives and address some of the world's most pressing challenges.

  • Promoting Technical Knowledge
  • Professional Development
  • Knowledge Sharing
  • Community Outreach
